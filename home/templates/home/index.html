Dear Students, Parents, and Well-Wishers,

Welcome to Home College for Girls, Ali Pur Chattha—an institution committed to academic excellence, character building, and empowering young women for a brighter future.

Since our establishment in 1992, we have been dedicated to providing quality education that nurtures intellectual growth, moral values, and leadership skills. Our well-equipped science and computer labs, library, and extracurricular activities create a holistic learning environment where students can excel in academics and personal development.

We take pride in our highly qualified faculty, outstanding student achievements, and a legacy of producing future leaders. Whether in Intermediate or Graduation programs, our goal is to provide every student with the knowledge, confidence, and skills needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving world.

We warmly welcome you to be part of our journey toward excellence. May Allah (SWT) bless us all with success in our endeavors.
